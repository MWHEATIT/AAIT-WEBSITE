Our Story

About Above All IT

We're a Sacramento-based IT consulting firm on a mission to make enterprise-grade technology accessible, reliable, and empowering for every business we serve.

Our Mission

Technology That Works as Hard as You Do

Above All IT was founded on a simple belief: every business deserves access to the kind of IT support that Fortune 500 companies take for granted: proactive, strategic, and always reliable. We exist to bridge that gap for small and mid-sized businesses across the Sacramento region.

We don't just fix computers. We become a strategic technology partner embedded in your operations, aligned with your goals, and invested in your long-term success. From day-to-day help desk support to multi-year technology roadmaps, we're with you every step of the way.

Our team of certified engineers and consultants brings decades of combined experience across industries including healthcare, legal, construction, finance, and professional services, so we understand the unique IT challenges your sector faces.

What Makes Above All IT Different

We've built our practice around the four things Sacramento businesses tell us they want most from an IT partner.

01

Rapid Response Times

When technology fails, every minute costs money. Our local Sacramento team maintains an average critical-issue response time under 15 minutes, with same-day on-site dispatch for issues that require a hands-on fix. We never leave you waiting in a ticket queue while your business stalls.

02

Tailored IT Strategies

We reject the one-size-fits-all approach. Before recommending anything, we invest time understanding your workflows, your team, your risk tolerance, and your budget. The result is an IT strategy that fits your business like a glove, adapting as you grow and evolve.

03

Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees. No surprise invoices. No upsells you didn't ask for. We quote clearly, bill predictably, and explain every line item in plain English. Our clients always know exactly what they're getting and what it costs, before we start any work.

04

Experienced Professionals

Our team holds certifications from Microsoft, Cisco, CompTIA, AWS, and more. But credentials are just the foundation — it's our real-world experience, industry knowledge, and genuine investment in your outcomes that truly sets us apart from the competition.
